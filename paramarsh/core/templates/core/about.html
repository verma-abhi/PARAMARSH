About Us

Our Mission

By PARAMARSH, our mission is to create an inclusive and interactive learning environment where students and mentors can connect seamlessly. We believe in the power of personalized education and strive to provide a platform that fosters meaningful interactions and impactful learning experiences.

What We Do

We offer an innovative online chat platform designed to bridge the gap between students and mentors. Whether you need help with homework, advice on career paths, or support with personal development, our platform enables you to connect with experienced mentors who are ready to assist you.

How It Works

  1. Join a Room: Enter your name and join a chat room dedicated to your topic of interest.
  2. Connect with Mentors: Engage in real-time conversations with mentors who can provide guidance and support.
  3. Interactive Features: Utilize our features such as message notifications, typing indicators, and more to enhance your communication experience.

Our Team

We are a team of passionate mentors and seniors dedicated to enhancing the educational experience. We mentors are carefully selected based on our expertise and commitment to helping students succeed under the Student Mentorship Program. Student Mentorship Program (SMP) is a program within the MNNIT Allahabad student community, with the primary objective of enabling constructive and positive interaction, guidance and mentorship of junior students by senior students. The Student Mentor is generally more experienced than the Mentee and makes use of that experience in a facilitative way to support the juniors for their development.
